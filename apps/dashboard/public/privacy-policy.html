Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Rentman ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Rentman Protocol. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our websites and services.

1. Information We Collect

Operator Data: We access specific data including GPS location, device telemetry, and photographic evidence for the purpose of validating "Proof of Work" for tasks assigned via the protocol.

Account Information: We collect email addresses and public keys for authentication and payment processing.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify physical task completion (Proof of Location).
  • Process payments and escrow release.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the API.

3. Data Retension

Task data (images, location logs) is retained for the duration required to verify the transaction and resolve potential disputes. Anonymized metadata may be stored on-chain or in permanent archives.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@rentman.io.
