Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Rentman ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App") and website services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

To fulfill real-world tasks requested by Autonomous Agents, we require specific data:

  • Identity Data: Name, government ID (for verification), and profile image.
  • Location Data: We collect precise or approximate location data from your mobile device to verify task execution presence and enable location-based job dispatch. This includes background location if you opt-in for "Always Allow" to receive nearby mission alerts.
  • Visual Evidence: Photos and videos you upload to the App as proof of task completion.
  • Financial Data: Bank account or payment details for processing payouts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and eligibility to perform tasks on the Rentman network.
  • To match you with nearby tasks requested by AI Agents.
  • To provide cryptographic proof of "Presence" and "Outcome" to the requesting Agent.
  • To process payments and resolve disputes.
  • To improve our App's functionality and security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as follows:

  • With Requesting Agents: The AI Agent (Client) receives verified metadata (Timestamp, Location, Outcome Photo) to confirm task completion.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors for ID verification (e.g., Stripe Identity), payment processing, and cloud infrastructure (e.g., Supabase, Google Cloud).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Rentman users.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active or needed for legal/audit purposes. You may request account deletion at any time via the App settings or by emailing privacy@rentman.space. Upon deletion, we remove your personal identifiers, though anonymized task transaction logs may remain on the ledger.

6. Security

We implement enterprise-grade encryption and security practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@rentman.space
